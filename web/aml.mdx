RugPullBot is committed to preventing the use of its platform for money laundering, terrorist financing, or any other illicit financial activity. This Anti-Money Laundering ("AML") and Know-Your-Customer ("KYC") Policy describes the measures we take and the obligations of our users.

## 1. Scope
This Policy applies to all users of RugPullBot and its associated services. By using the platform you agree to comply with this Policy and all applicable AML / CFT (Counter-Financing of Terrorism) laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct
You may not use RugPullBot to:
- Launder proceeds of crime or conceal the origin of illicit funds.
- Finance terrorism or any sanctioned activity.
- Evade sanctions, or transact with sanctioned individuals, entities, or jurisdictions.
- Use funds derived from fraud, theft, or other unlawful activity.

## 3. Customer Due Diligence
Where required by law, or where activity warrants, we may:
- Request identifying information to verify your identity (KYC).
- Screen users against applicable sanctions, PEP (Politically Exposed Person), and watchlists.
- Decline, suspend, or terminate service to anyone who fails verification or presents unacceptable risk.

## 4. Transaction Monitoring
We monitor on-platform activity for patterns indicative of money laundering or fraud, including unusual volume, structuring, or transactions linked to high-risk wallets or sources.

## 5. Sanctions Compliance
We do not knowingly provide services to individuals or entities located in, or subject to, comprehensive sanctions (including those administered by OFAC, the UN, the EU, or other relevant authorities). Access from such jurisdictions is prohibited.

## 6. Reporting
We cooperate with law enforcement and regulators and may report suspicious activity to the appropriate authorities as required by law, without prior notice to the user.

## 7. Record Keeping
We retain records of user information and activity for the period required by applicable law.

## 8. User Obligations
You represent that the funds you use are from legitimate sources and that your use of the platform complies with all applicable laws. You must promptly provide any information we reasonably request for compliance purposes.

## 9. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

## 10. Contact
For AML / KYC inquiries, contact us on [Telegram](https://t.me/theonlyrugger) or email support@rugpullbot.com.
