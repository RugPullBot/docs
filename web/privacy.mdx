RugPullBot ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information and responsibilities when using our bot and services.

## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with our services, such as your IP address, device type, and activity logs.
- **Bot Commands:** We log certain commands sent to the bot to enhance functionality and provide support.

## 2. Seed Phrases
**We do not store or collect any seed phrases generated by the bot.** It is solely the user's responsibility to:
- Store their seed phrases securely.
- Back them up in a reliable location.

Failure to do so could result in permanent loss of access to associated wallets and funds. We disclaim all responsibility for lost or mishandled seed phrases.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
The information we collect is used to:
- Provide and improve our services.
- Respond to user inquiries or support requests.
- Analyze usage trends to enhance performance and user experience.

## 4. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. User Responsibility
Users acknowledge that they are responsible for safeguarding their accounts, credentials, and seed phrases. Misuse of the bot or failure to comply with these guidelines may result in loss of access or data.

## 6. Changes to Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us on [Telegram](https://t.me/theonlyrugger) or email [support@rugpullbot.com](mailto:support@rugpullbot.com).
