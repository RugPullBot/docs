RugPullBot ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Bot and services.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Usage Data:** Information about your interactions, such as your IP address, device type, and activity logs.
- **Bot Commands:** We log commands sent to the Bot to improve functionality and support.

## 2. Seed Phrases
We do not store or collect any seed phrases generated by the Bot. It is your responsibility to secure and back them up.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and enhance our services.
- To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
- To analyze usage trends and improve the user experience.

## 4. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no method is completely secure.

## 5. User Responsibility
You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials and any sensitive data. We are not liable for any losses due to your negligence.

## 6. Changes to the Privacy Policy
This Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot signifies acceptance of the revised policy.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns, contact us on [Telegram](https://t.me/theonlyrugger) or email support@rugpullbot.com.
